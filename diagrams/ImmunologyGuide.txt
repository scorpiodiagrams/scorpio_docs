##ðŸ›¡Immune System
The immune system is a complex and efficient.  It's important for its role in health.  It responds to damage, viruses, bacteria, toxins that would otherwise cause long term damage to the body. Viruses and bacteria that damage the body need to evade the immune system.  To understand immunity we need to understand both what the immune system is doing and what bacteria and viruses do to evade it.
